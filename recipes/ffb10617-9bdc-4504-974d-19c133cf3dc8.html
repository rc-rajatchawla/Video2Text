Creamy Chicken Pasta with Peas

This recipe is a delightful combination of tender chicken pieces, sweet peas, and pasta enveloped in a light creamy sauce. It's a comforting and easy-to-make dish that's perfect for a weeknight dinner.

Step 1

Gather all the ingredients: boneless chicken breast, pasta of your choice, fresh or frozen peas, garlic, onion, chicken stock, cream, olive oil, salt, and pepper.

Step 2

Cut the chicken breast into bite-sized pieces and season with salt and pepper.

Step 3

Heat a couple of tablespoons of olive oil in a large pan over medium-high heat and saut the chicken pieces until they are golden brown and cooked through. Remove the chicken from the pan and set aside.

Step 4

In the same pan, add a little more olive oil if needed, and saut a finely chopped onion until translucent. Add minced garlic and cook for another minute until fragrant.

Step 5

Pour in about a cup of chicken stock and bring to a simmer, scraping up any browned bits from the bottom of the pan.

Step 6

Add the peas to the pan and cook until they are just tender, which will take about 3-4 minutes for fresh peas or 1-2 minutes for frozen peas.

Step 7

While the peas are cooking, boil the pasta in a separate pot according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.

Step 8

Reduce the heat under the pan with the peas to low and stir in half a cup of cream, letting it warm through without boiling.

Step 9

Return the cooked chicken to the pan and add the drained pasta. Toss everything together gently, allowing the pasta to absorb the flavors of the sauce.

Step 10

Season the pasta with additional salt and pepper to taste. If the sauce is too thick, a splash of pasta water or chicken stock can be added to reach the desired consistency.

Step 11

Serve the creamy chicken pasta with peas hot, garnished with grated Parmesan cheese or fresh herbs if desired.
