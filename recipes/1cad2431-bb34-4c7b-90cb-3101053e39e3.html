Creamy Chicken Pasta with Peas

This recipe brings together tender chicken, sweet peas, and pasta in a light creamy sauce, seasoned with Italian herbs and Parmesan cheese. It's a simple yet satisfying dish that's perfect for a weeknight dinner.

Step 1

Start by bringing a large pot of salted water to a boil. Add 12 ounces of your preferred pasta shape and cook according to the package instructions until al dente. Reserve 1 cup of pasta water before draining.

Step 2

While the pasta cooks, cut 1 pound of boneless, skinless chicken breasts into bite-sized pieces. Season with salt, pepper, and a teaspoon of Italian seasoning.

Step 3

Heat 2 tablespoons of olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Add the chicken pieces and saut until golden brown and cooked through, about 5-7 minutes. Remove the chicken from the skillet and set aside.

Step 4

In the same skillet, add 1 cup of diced onions and saut until translucent. Add 2 cloves of minced garlic and cook for an additional minute until fragrant.

Step 5

Stir in 1 cup of fresh or frozen peas and cook for 2-3 minutes until the peas are tender.

Step 6

Reduce the heat to low and add 1/2 cup of heavy cream and 1/2 cup of grated Parmesan cheese to the skillet, stirring until the cheese is melted and the sauce is creamy.

Step 7

Return the cooked chicken to the skillet and add the drained pasta. Toss everything together, adding a little reserved pasta water if needed to loosen the sauce.

Step 8

Season the pasta with additional salt, pepper, and Italian seasoning to taste. Cook for another 2 minutes on low heat, allowing the flavors to meld.

Step 9

Serve the creamy chicken pasta with peas hot, garnished with extra Parmesan cheese and a sprinkle of chopped parsley or basil for a fresh touch.
