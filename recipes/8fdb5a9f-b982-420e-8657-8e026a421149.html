Garlic Spinach Pasta

This dish combines the earthy flavors of spinach with the aromatic punch of garlic, all tossed with al dente pasta and finished with parmesan cheese for a simple yet satisfying meal.

Step 1

Begin by boiling half a pound of your preferred pasta shape in salted water until it is al dente, usually around 8 to 10 minutes depending on the pasta type.

Step 2

While the pasta cooks, finely chop two cloves of garlic and set aside.

Step 3

Heat a large skillet over medium heat with two tablespoons of olive oil, then add the chopped garlic to the skillet, being careful not to let it burn.

Step 4

Once the garlic is fragrant, add four cups of fresh spinach to the skillet in batches, allowing each batch to wilt slightly before adding the next.

Step 5

Stir the spinach frequently to coat it with the garlic-infused oil and to ensure even cooking.

Step 6

Season the spinach with salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste.

Step 7

Once the pasta is cooked, reserve a cup of the pasta water before draining.

Step 8

Add the drained pasta to the skillet with the spinach and toss to combine, adding a splash of the reserved pasta water to help the sauce coat the pasta evenly.

Step 9

Grate half a cup of parmesan cheese and add it to the pasta, tossing everything together until the cheese is melted and the pasta is well coated.

Step 10

Serve the pasta hot, with additional parmesan cheese and a drizzle of olive oil if desired.
