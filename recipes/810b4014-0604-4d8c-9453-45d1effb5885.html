Simple Spinach Pasta for Two

This recipe creates a delightful Spinach Pasta dish that is both easy to prepare and full of flavor. Fresh spinach is sauted with garlic and tossed with al dente pasta, parmesan cheese, and a touch of olive oil for a satisfying meal suitable for two people.

Step 1

Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil and cook half a pound of your chosen pasta according to the package instructions until al dente, usually between 8 to 10 minutes.

Step 2

While the pasta is cooking, finely chop two cloves of garlic and set aside.

Step 3

Heat a large skillet over medium heat and add two tablespoons of olive oil, then add the chopped garlic, sauting it just until fragrant, about 1 minute, being careful not to let it burn.

Step 4

Add half a pound of fresh, washed spinach to the skillet with the garlic, tossing it gently to coat with the oil and garlic.

Step 5

Season the spinach with a pinch of salt and pepper, and continue to saut until the spinach is wilted, about 2 to 3 minutes.

Step 6

Once the pasta is cooked, reserve a cup of the pasta cooking water before draining the pasta.

Step 7

Add the drained pasta to the skillet with the sauted spinach and garlic, tossing everything to combine.

Step 8

Gradually add a few tablespoons of the reserved pasta water to the skillet, just enough to create a light sauce that coats the pasta.

Step 9

Grate a quarter cup of fresh parmesan cheese over the pasta and spinach, and toss to melt the cheese and bind the sauce.

Step 10

Finish the dish with a final drizzle of olive oil, adjust seasoning with salt and pepper if necessary, and serve immediately.
