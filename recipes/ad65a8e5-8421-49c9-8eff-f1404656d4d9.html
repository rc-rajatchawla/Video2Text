Homemade Spinach Pasta with Garlic and Olive Oil

This recipe features homemade spinach pasta, which is paired with a simple yet delicious garlic and olive oil sauce. The fresh pasta dough is infused with pureed spinach, giving it a vibrant green color and a subtle spinach flavor. The sauce is made with sauted garlic and high-quality olive oil, finished with grated Parmesan cheese for a touch of richness.

Step 1

Begin by blanching 2 cups of fresh spinach leaves in boiling water for 30 seconds, then immediately plunge them into ice water to stop the cooking process. Drain and squeeze out as much water as possible.

Step 2

Puree the blanched spinach in a food processor until smooth. Add 2 large eggs and blend until fully incorporated.

Step 3

Place 2 cups of all-purpose flour on a clean work surface, make a well in the center, and pour the spinach and egg mixture into the well. Gradually incorporate the flour into the liquid using a fork, starting from the inside and working your way out.

Step 4

Once the dough starts to come together, knead it with your hands for about 5 to 10 minutes until it becomes smooth and elastic. Wrap the dough in plastic wrap and let it rest for at least 30 minutes.

Step 5

After resting, divide the dough into four pieces. Roll out each piece using a pasta machine or a rolling pin until it reaches the desired thickness, then cut into your preferred pasta shape.

Step 6

Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Cook the pasta for 2 to 3 minutes, or until al dente. Drain and set aside, reserving 1 cup of the pasta water.

Step 7

While the pasta cooks, heat 3 tablespoons of extra virgin olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add 3 cloves of minced garlic and saut until fragrant, being careful not to burn it.

Step 8

Add the cooked pasta to the skillet with the garlic and toss to coat. If the pasta seems dry, add a little of the reserved pasta water to achieve a saucy consistency.

Step 9

Season the pasta with salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste. Stir in a handful of freshly grated Parmesan cheese until melted and evenly distributed.

Step 10

Serve the pasta hot, garnished with additional Parmesan cheese and a drizzle of olive oil, if desired.
