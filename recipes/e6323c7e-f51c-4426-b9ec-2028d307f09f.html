Chicken Pasta with Peas

This recipe is a delightful combination of tender chicken pieces, al dente pasta, and sweet green peas, all brought together with a light and creamy sauce. It's a perfect weeknight meal that is both satisfying and easy to prepare.

Step 1

Gather all ingredients: boneless chicken breast, pasta (such as penne or fusilli), fresh or frozen peas, garlic, onions, chicken stock, cream, olive oil, salt, pepper, and Parmesan cheese.

Step 2

Cut the chicken breast into bite-sized pieces, season with salt and pepper, and set aside.

Step 3

Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil and cook the pasta according to package instructions until al dente. In the last 2 minutes of cooking, add the peas to the boiling pasta water. Drain and set aside, reserving a cup of the pasta water.

Step 4

While the pasta is cooking, heat a couple of tablespoons of olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Add the seasoned chicken pieces and saut until they are golden brown and cooked through. Remove the chicken from the skillet and set aside.

Step 5

In the same skillet, add a bit more olive oil if needed, and saut finely chopped onions and minced garlic until translucent and fragrant.

Step 6

Deglaze the skillet with a splash of chicken stock, scraping up any browned bits from the bottom of the skillet for extra flavor.

Step 7

Reduce the heat to medium and stir in a cup of cream, bringing the mixture to a gentle simmer.

Step 8

Return the cooked chicken to the skillet, add the drained pasta and peas, and toss everything together, adding a bit of the reserved pasta water if needed to loosen the sauce.

Step 9

Season the pasta with additional salt and pepper to taste, and stir in grated Parmesan cheese until the sauce is creamy and coats the pasta evenly.

Step 10

Serve the Chicken Pasta with Peas hot, garnished with extra Parmesan cheese and a sprinkle of fresh herbs, such as parsley or basil, if desired.
